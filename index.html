About Us

Apple Connections (Appleconn) cc is a South African , value-added, business-to business (B2B) distribution services firm focusing on the markets for Maintenance, Repair & Operations materials specialising in pumps, valves, motors, actuators, conveyor accessories, bulk bags and instrumentation equipment.

As these markets are extremely diverse and highly complex, many opportunities exist for Appleconn to provide innovative solutions to customers needs.

The Company co-ordinates product and information flows, adding value and providing significant cost savings to both end-users and suppliers while supporting customer initiatives with superior industry knowledge and technical expertise.

Our goal is to obtain a leading position in its field in the Southern African B2B markets.

Our people work continuously to understand your needs because we recognise that every organisation is different and each has its own requirements and methods of working. It's this approach coupled with our wealth of industry knowledge and technical expertise that makes the difference. For all our customers every day, Apple connection people put their skills and experience to good use to make procurement easier, simpler and more cost effective.

We are committed to providing induction programmes, continuous opportunities for feedback and high quality training and education for our people. Regular briefing sessions are also held at each location where information from around the business is discussed.

Appleconn cc is based in Rustenburg with a branch in johannesburg.

Our company operates through well-established and reputable brands. Through this structure we aim to become your supply partner of choice, by understanding and fulfilling your individual needs and delivering excellence across every facet of our business.

Appleconn is striving to becoming one of South Africa’s leading distributors with a network of strategically located branches in Rustenburg, Johannesburg and Botswana. We handle a full profile of a wide range of products, of which the majority are available for same or next day delivery.

Established in 2009 Appleconn also operates as a supply chain partner to most mining companies in South Africa.

Suffice to say that we are heading to being one of the Country’s leading distributors, with an unrivalled reputation for customer service. We are also widely respected in the industrial sector for supplying Maintenance, Repair and Operations (MRO) requirements.
